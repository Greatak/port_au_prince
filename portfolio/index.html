CDFA Survey Map

Ohio

Preview Page View Source

Part of Public Participation coursework, this project consisted initially of a census of Ohio government officials to determine what issues were facing communities in Ohio to allow the CDFA to better serve their purpose.

This is part of our final analysis, an interactive map to plot the results of the survey. It's a custom GIS solution to track responses in a generalized format, anonymized to a county level. Dedicated efforts were made to ensure cross-browswer performance.

TMACOG Resiliency Study

Toledo, Ohio

Preview Page View Source

Toledo is one of the Rust Belt's minor cities, but equally crippled from the economic transition that moved industry away from the area. However, it has proven successful in reviving a fledgling industry in solar energy and reorganizing transportation networks. The regional development organization was the subject of this investigation.

Though this lacks any significant technical work, it was prototyped in a new style with a presentation tool currently under development.
